  5. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?
    6.

    My tip-of-the-week is a quote about waking up in the morning from the book "Meditations" by Marcus Aurelius: "In the morning, when you rise unwillingly, let this thought be present: I am rising to the work of the human being. Why then am I dissatisfied if I am going to do the things for which I exist and for which I was brought into the world? [...] Do you not see the little plants, the little birds, the ants, the spiders, the bees working together to put in order their separate parts of the universe? And are you unwilling to do the work of a himan being, and do you not make haste to do that which is according to your nature?" So when you don't feel like getting out of bed in the morning, just remember that you have the work of a human to do and a universe to contribute to.
