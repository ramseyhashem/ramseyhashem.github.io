  5. What's your experience of the class?
    6.

    My first impression of the class is pretty favorable! After taking CS429 and CS439, both of which had two-hour lectures, it has felt pretty refreshing to be taking a CS class that is only an hour as opposed to two. I also like Professor Downing's teaching style involving taking notes as class progresses. In the past, I've found it difficult to balance listening to lecture and taking notes, especially when the information is all on a powerpoint that I can just download after. So Dr. Downing's note-taking has been a helpful way for me to keep up with the most important concepts and questions to keep in mind.
