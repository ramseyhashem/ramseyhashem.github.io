  5. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?
    6.

    My pick-of-the-week is Pixar in a Box! PIAB is an awesome online set of lessons in which Pixar has teamed up with Khan Academy to take you through their production pipeline for their films and explain the underlying math and science that they then program into their tools. As someone with an interest in working in Computer Graphics, I've found Pixar in a Box to be incredibly interesting and enjoyable.
